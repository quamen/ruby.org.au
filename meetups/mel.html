Melbourne Ruby

When

Melbourne Ruby meetings happen on the last Wednesday of every month (with the exception of December which has a social event sometime in the middle of the month).

Melbourne Ruby hack nights (where the focus is more on coding than talking) happen on the second Tuesday of every month.

All events are listed on Meetup.com.

Where

Inspire9
Level 1, 41-43 Stewart St
Richmond
6:00pm for a 6:30pm start!

Organisers

  • Mario Visic (@mariovisic)
  • Pat Allan (@pat)

Meeting Format

  • News & events
  • Who's hiring, who's available
  • Lightning talks, tutorials and presentations on Ruby and related topics
  • Food and drinks provided

Sponsors

Get Involved

Chatter about upcoming meetings takes place on Meetup and the Ruby or Rails Oceania Google Group.

Presenters, organisers and sponsors are always welcome, contact the organisers through the Google Group or on Twitter.
